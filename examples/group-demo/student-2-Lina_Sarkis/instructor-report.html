11 · Final interpretation

The diagnostic measures current evidence, not full potential. Strong performance reduces remediation but never removes SAT-specific teaching; weak performance increases guided instruction; limited coverage prevents unjustified reductions, and unassessed lessons are treated as unknown. Course-budget changes are assigned to explicit components. Lesson allocations retain two-decimal precision. Reading & Writing, Math, and strategy totals are each rounded upward to the next whole hour, and the final scheduled total is the sum of those three rounded categories.
